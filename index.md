## What is boudoir photography?

Boudoir photography is intimate, sensual portraiture — typically in lingerie, robes, or implied nude — that captures a woman as she is, not as she's been told to be. At Mark Andrew Boudoir, every shoot is a full-day, in-studio experience designed for women who don't think of themselves as "the photogenic type." Most leave saying it was the most powerful thing they've ever done for themselves.

## An experience built around you

## How it works

1. **Consultation.** A 10-minute phone call or Zoom. We talk through your vibe, your vision, and what's going to make you feel like the most magnetic version of yourself. Zero pressure.
2. **Studio day.** A full day in the studio. Professional hair and makeup, multiple wardrobe changes, head-to-toe posing direction. Every shot is posed by Mark — you never have to "figure out what to do."
3. **Same-day reveal.** You see your images the same day, on a 75-inch screen, ordered into a curated story. Choose what you want to keep — albums, wall art, digitals.
