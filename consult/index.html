The Honest Answer

What is boudoir, actually?

A professional portrait experience where you're the entire point. Old-Hollywood glamour — bold lighting, statement jewelry, painted backdrops, fur, gowns. Timeless, never trendy. Our rule: these should look so good your granddaughter someday says, "look how good Grandma looked."

✓

No modeling experience needed

Mark poses every client head to toe in every single shot — hands, chin, hips, breath. You never guess what to do.

✓

Every body. Every age. 18–80.

There's no "boudoir body." Posing and light are our job, not yours. Most clients have never done anything like this.

✓

Tasteful by default

Classy and glamorous is the baseline — you decide how spicy it gets. Photos stay private unless you say otherwise.
