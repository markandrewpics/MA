The Process

Four steps from booking to album in hand. Designed so you arrive ready, leave celebrated.

01

Vision Planning Call

A 30-minute video call covering wardrobe, color palette, props, and every detail of your shoot day. By the time you arrive, the entire experience is mapped.

02

Hair & Makeup

Aletheia Jean transforms you in the studio chair — full glam, lashes, and styling. She stays through the entire shoot for every touch-up.

03

The Photoshoot

Three outfits, head-to-toe posing direction, and a private studio. We start tame and build to your "wow" outfit. You'll never wonder what to do next.

04

Same-Day Reveal

After lunch, you return to see every retouched image for the first time — and walk out with your album, wall art, and prints designed.

meet

The man behind the lens

Mark Andrew is an award-winning boudoir photographer who has photographed clients internationally over the last decade. With thousands of women through his studios in South Bend, Indiana and Beaufort, South Carolina, his luxury experience is built around full-day sessions, complete head-to-toe posing direction, and same-day image reveals.

Beyond the studio, Mark is a respected educator and speaker on boudoir photography — helping photographers around the country hone their craft, build their businesses, and elevate their work for the modern client.

He believes every woman deserves to be photographed the way she wants to be remembered — and that the most powerful images come from clients who never thought of themselves as photogenic.

Frequently Asked

A few of the questions clients ask before booking. For full pricing, wardrobe, and pre-session details, see the blog.

Is boudoir photography awkward?
No. The studio is private, the team is small (just me and Aletheia), and I direct every pose. Almost every client arrives nervous and leaves standing differently. Read the full answer →
How much does a boudoir session cost?
Most first-time clients invest $1,500 to $2,500 total — $499 booking retainer, $200 hair and makeup, plus what you choose to take home at the same-day reveal. Full pricing breakdown →
What should I wear to my boudoir shoot?
We plan wardrobe in detail during your free vision planning call. Most clients bring 3 to 5 outfits across three looks — and the studio offers full guidance. Wardrobe guide →
Do I need modeling experience?
None. I direct every pose — your hands, your chin, your legs, every angle. The women in the portfolio are everyday clients, not models. Posing direction is the entire point.
Are my photos kept private?
Yes. By default only you and I see your images — not even your makeup artist sees them without your explicit consent. Galleries are private and password-protected.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes — Affirm, Klarna, and Afterpay at checkout (6 months interest-free up to 36 months), plus an in-house 6-month interest-free plan with $1,000 down on orders over $1,000.
