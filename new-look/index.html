From arrival to reveal

EVERY DETAIL,
A PLEASURE.

9:00 AM — Arrival

Hair & Makeup

You arrive to coffee and calm. Aletheia builds your look with you — camera-ready soft glam or bold maximalism — while the studio warms up around you. No rushing. This day belongs to you.

11:00 AM — The Shoot

Three Sets, Ninety Minutes

Three outfits across three fully styled sets — painted backdrops, fur, gowns, statement jewelry. I direct every pose head to toe, so you never have to wonder what to do with your hands.

12:30 PM — Lunch

You Relax. I Finish.

I shoot print-ready in camera, so while you have lunch, the editing wraps. Most studios make you wait two to four weeks to see anything. Here, the wait is about an hour.

1:00 PM — The Reveal

The Same-Day Reveal

130–160 fully edited images on a 65-inch screen in 4K — the same afternoon. This is the moment women talk about for years: seeing yourself the way the people who love you always have.
