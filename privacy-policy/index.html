Privacy policy.

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy is critical to us. This policy outlines the types of information we collect from visitors, how we use that information, the steps we take to protect it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using markandrewboudoir.com, you consent to the practices described below.

01.Purpose of collection

Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which the information is being collected. We collect personal data only when it is reasonably necessary to provide the services you request — such as scheduling a consultation, processing a session booking, sending email communications, or responding to inquiries.

02.Limited use

Personal information is collected only for the specific purposes identified at the time of collection. We will not use or disclose your information for any other purpose without your explicit consent, unless required to do so by law.

03.Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements, and resolve any disputes. After that period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

04.Collection methods

We gather individual data by legal and reasonable means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned. Information may be collected directly through forms you submit, indirectly through cookies and analytics tools, or from third-party services you authorize to share data with us (such as your booking platform or payment processor).

05.Data quality

Personal information should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and should be accurate, complete, and current to the extent necessary for those purposes. You may request to review, correct, or update any personal information we hold about you at any time.

06.Data security

We protect your personal data with industry-standard security measures against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or alteration. Nevertheless, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure — and while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

07.Access and transparency

We will make readily available to clients and visitors information about our policies and procedures regarding the management of personal information. You may contact us at any time to request access to the data we hold about you, ask questions about how it is used, or request its deletion.

08.Our commitment

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles to ensure that the privacy of personal information is protected and maintained. This Privacy Policy is subject to change without notice, and we encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

09.Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, the way we handle your personal data, or wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us at mark@markandrewboudoir.com.
