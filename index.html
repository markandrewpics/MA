What is boudoir?

01

What is a boudoir session?

A boudoir session is an intimate portrait experience — a luxury studio shoot designed to capture you the way you want to be seen, not just how you happen to look in passing photos.

02

What should I expect during a boudoir session?

A full-day experience. Professional hair & makeup with Aletheia, head-to-toe posing direction with Mark, and a same-day reveal of your gallery before you leave the studio.

03

Who is boudoir for?

Every body. Every age. Women, men, couples — for birthdays, milestones, weddings, divorces, or "just because." If you've thought about it, it's already for you.

04

Who books a boudoir session in South Bend?

Anyone who wants to be photographed the way they want to be remembered. Mark Andrew Boudoir works with clients of every body type, age, and background across South Bend, Mishawaka, Granger, Elkhart, Goshen, Niles, and the broader Michiana area.

A decade of work

An experience built around you.

Mark Andrew is an award-winning boudoir photographer who has photographed clients internationally over the last decade. With thousands of women through his studios in South Bend, Indiana and Beaufort, South Carolina, his entire experience is built around one outcome — transforming the woman who has never felt photogenic into the woman she didn't know she could be: confident, undeniable, and deeply seen.

Beyond the studio, Mark is a respected voice in modern boudoir photography — invited to speak at industry events, teach at conferences, and help raise the bar for what this experience can mean to the women on the other side of the lens.

He believes every person deserves to be photographed the way they want to be remembered.

The full experience

What to expect with your boudoir experience.

Pro hair & makeup

Full glam by Aletheia Jean — built specifically for how you photograph. She stays through the entire shoot for touch-ups and on-set assistance, so you never have to think about it.

Expert wardrobe styling

Mark personally helps you choose, layer, and style every look — from your own pieces or new ones you bring in — so each outfit photographs the way you want to be seen.

Head-to-toe posing

Complete direction the entire session. Mark poses for every body type — finding the angles, lines, and shapes that flatter yours specifically. You'll never wonder where to put your hands.

Same-day reveal

You'll see your gallery the same day you shoot it. No anxious waiting, no surprises — just you, the screen, and the images.
