What is boudoir?

01

What is a boudoir session?

A boudoir session is an intimate portrait experience — a luxury studio shoot designed to capture you the way you want to be seen, not just how you happen to look in passing photos.

02

What should I expect during a boudoir session?

A full-day experience. Head-to-toe posing direction with Mark and a same-day reveal of your gallery before you leave the studio — with optional hair & makeup available through Aletheia.

03

Who is boudoir for?

Every body. Every age. Women, men, couples — for birthdays, milestones, weddings, divorces, or "just because." If you've thought about it, it's already for you.

04

Who books a boudoir session in South Bend?

Anyone who wants to be photographed the way they want to be remembered. Mark Andrew Boudoir works with clients of every body type, age, and background across South Bend, Mishawaka, Granger, Elkhart, Goshen, Niles, and the broader Michiana area.
